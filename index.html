Sedona, Arizona

Highlights from our recent trip ✈️

This past spring break we flew out to Arizona to visit family and explore as many sites as we could during the few days we had there. The place that really stood out to me and I can't wait to get back to is Sedona, Arizona.

What made Sedona so great

  • The landscape! The red rock formations were amazing.
  • Trails
  • Pink Jeep Tour was so fun for the whole family
  • Ate at some great restaurants

The second day that we were there, it rained the whole time. Determined to not let that keep us from exploring, one of the places we visited was the
Red Rock Ranger Vistor Center in Sedona. It was a great place to stop at when wanting to stay out of the rain. There were some interactive activities
that my kids enjoyed and where it was located, great views.
